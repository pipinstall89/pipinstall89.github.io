0x1 About Me

Ironically, I have never been an "Apple Fan Boy." Sure, I've always used an iPhone, but I didn't get my first MacBook till my first IT job provided me one. I have mixed feelings about Steve Jobs, don't own AirPods, and don't own Apple stock.

Why Apple? Specifically, macOS?

Because I got a reverse shell to work on my Macbook and I've been hooked to Apple since.

Additionally, Apple's marketing team did a great job at convincing the world that Macs don't get viruses. Second reason, I got tired of pentesting web apps. Third reason, so much of the today's offensive security focuses on web and Windows that a part of me felt like Apple stuff was ripe for the picking (pun intended.)

Currently, I am working through OffSec's OSMR course and reading Patrick Wardle's book, 'The Art of Mac Malware.' My goal for this site is to serve as a collection for myself and others for all things macOS security.

Thanks for the read,
